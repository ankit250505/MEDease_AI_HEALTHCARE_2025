Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Creating an account is simple and secure. Click "Start Your Checkup" on our homepage, then follow the prompts to enter your basic information including name, email, and phone number. You'll receive a verification code to confirm your account, and you'll be ready to begin your health journey with Med Ease.

For the most accurate analysis, provide details about your current symptoms, their duration and severity, any medications you're taking, relevant medical history, and recent test results or doctor visits. You can also upload photos, lab reports, or medical documents to enhance the assessment.

Appointments & Healthcare Providers

Based on your symptom analysis and health profile, our AI recommends the most appropriate type of healthcare provider - whether that's a general practitioner, specialist, urgent care, or emergency room. We provide a curated list of nearby providers with ratings, availability, and contact information.

Currently, Med Ease provides you with direct contact information and links to provider booking systems. We're working on integrated appointment booking features that will allow you to schedule visits directly through our platform, which will be available in early 2026.

If our analysis indicates urgent symptoms, Med Ease will immediately recommend seeking emergency care and provide you with the nearest emergency room locations. For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 or your local emergency number immediately. Med Ease is designed to complement, not replace, emergency medical services.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. Med Ease is fully HIPAA-compliant and uses enterprise-grade encryption to protect all your data. We employ multiple layers of security including end-to-end encryption, secure data centers, regular security audits, and strict access controls. Your health information is never shared without your explicit consent.

Only you have access to your complete medical profile. Our AI systems process your data to provide analysis, but this is done using anonymized, encrypted information. Healthcare providers only receive the information you explicitly choose to share with them. Our support team can only access limited, non-medical account information when helping you with technical issues.

Technical Support

Med Ease works best on modern browsers including Chrome (version 90+), Firefox (version 88+), Safari (version 14+), and Edge (version 90+). We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience and security. Internet Explorer is not supported.

Ensure your documents are in supported formats (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOCX) and under 10MB each. Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our support team with details about your browser and device.

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a secure reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder. The reset link expires after 1 hour for security purposes.
